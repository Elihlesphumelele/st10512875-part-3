Apex Group Expands Across South Africa

Apex Group, the parent company of Apex Protect, continues expanding within South Africa’s financial sector through strategic acquisitions, including Currency News and MiningMX.

Innovation Update

Launch of Apex Protect Insurance Platform

Apex Group has introduced Apex Protect, a modern fund-insurance platform combining Directors & Officers (D&O) coverage with ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) incentives.

In partnership with Mosaic Insurance and Aon, the platform delivers instant pricing, faster turnaround times, and premium rewards for businesses improving ESG performance.

This innovation provides clients with a transparent and efficient way to purchase fund-level D&O insurance.
